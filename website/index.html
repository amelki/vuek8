Your clusters at a glance.

A fast, lightweight Kubernetes dashboard that shows your nodes, pods, and logs in real time. Single binary, no bloat.

Features

◎

Topology View

Visual map of nodes and pods. See how your workloads are distributed across the cluster at a glance.

☍

Multi-Cluster

Auto-discovers all kubeconfig files. Switch between clusters instantly with a single click.

☰

Live Logs

Stream pod logs in real-time. Search, filter, and download. No kubectl required.

⚡

Real-Time

Server-sent events keep everything in sync. Pod status, node metrics, and more — always up to date.

⚙

Lightweight

Single binary, no Electron. Native desktop window with minimal resource usage. Under 30 MB.

↷

Zero Config

Just launch it. Vue.k8 reads your existing kubeconfig files and connects automatically.
